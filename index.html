Why it exists

Maintenance tracking usually fragments fast.

Asset ownership changes. Equipment moves between people. Photos live in a phone. Parts research sits in another tab. WrenchLog puts those threads into one workflow, so you can see the story of a machine without digging through scattered reminders.

It is useful when you are juggling multiple projects, sharing tools or vehicles with other people, or trying to remember exactly what was replaced, adjusted, or inspected before the next round of work begins.
